Final Project

"Chaos Generator"

move the cursor around change various effects, click the switch to forward the progression. 
as the progressionn forwards, changes are made based on various seed data at that second such as cursor position and frame count.
this ensures no playthrough sounds the same.  

the cursor is controled by a joystick through the arduino.  

sequences in the progression control the led's on the arduino.
